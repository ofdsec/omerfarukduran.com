About Me

Who I Am

I am a cybersecurity professional with over 6 years of hands-on experience and more than 10 years of active involvement in the field.

I have extensive experience in penetration testing across Web, Mobile, API, Network, Active Directory, Wi-Fi, Cloud, and AI environments, as well as vulnerability management, source code analysis, and Red Teaming operations.

I build, fine-tune, and orchestrate custom AI agents and LLMs for offensive security workflows — from automated vulnerability triage and intelligent attack surface mapping to AI-driven red team operations. I deploy and manage local LLM infrastructure, develop RAG-based security pipelines, and integrate AI capabilities into every stage of the security lifecycle.

On the defensive side, I manage EDR, SIEM, PAM, DLP, and WAF solutions, handle incident response and threat hunting operations, and enforce infrastructure hardening across enterprise environments.

Currently serving as a Cybersecurity Specialist at A101, Turkey's largest retail chain, where I am responsible for end-to-end security management of the entire infrastructure.

Recognized on the Hall of Fame lists of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and the U.S. Department of the Interior (DOI) for discovered security vulnerabilities.

I hold the eWPTXv2, eMAPT, MCRTA, and CAP certifications in offensive security and application security.
